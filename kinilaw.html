Kilawing Tanguige Recipe

Indulge in the zesty and refreshing flavors of kilawing tanigue. Made with the freshest ingredients, this dish is the perfect choice for seafood lovers.

How to cook:

  1. Get yourself a nice clean bowl and combine tanguigue with a cup of cane vinegar. Mix well and just discard excess liquid.
  2. Get another bowl and mix the remaining 1 cup of cane vinegar with onion, ginger, and chilies. Just add the tanguigue, 3-5 minutes before serving them, then mix well.
  3. Finally, add the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original and season well according to your preference. And that's our dish. Top this on bread, eat as is or pair with your favourite meat dish. There's something about that sour and salty taste that will make you fall in love with this dish.
